About Me

I am from Norfolk, Nebraska. I am a junior at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln majoring in Advertising and Public Relations. I believe that connections and bonds with people around you are an important aspect in our lives. Because of that reason I thought jump starting my college career by joining greek life. I am currently serve as an active member of Pi Beta Phi. During my free time I love giving back to the communities. I love working with children, so I work as a paraprofessional substitute in Elementary Schools, I help with children's volleyball and soccer camps, and volunteer at a Title 1 school in Lincoln. My goals after college are along the Public Relations track. I want to begin as a PR specialist to learn what it is like in the real world within this career. After getting my feet under me, I want to start a PR firm.
